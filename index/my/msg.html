Company Profile
[Bud Light]

Bud Light was born in the United States in 1982. It is an internationally renowned high-end beer brand under the Anheuser-Busch Group. It is loved by countless young people around the world for its young, fashionable, free and refreshing image.

Bud Light is committed to becoming the "blue friend" of the younger generation - not only a sponsor of top international sports events such as the NFL and NBA, but also shines in the music and two-dimensional fields that consumers like. It has held the Bud Light Dive Bar Summer Music Festival series tour for top European and American musicians such as Lady Gaga and Post Malone, and has also cooperated with well-known film and television IP "Game of Thrones" and well-known e-sports team Fnatic.

From 2016 to 2018, Bud Light ranked first in the world's most valuable beer brand for three consecutive years. Among the top 50 beer brands in the world in 2021 released by Brand Finance, Bud Light ranked fifth.

[Brand Positioning]

Budweiser Blue closely follows the values ​​of young consumers, embraces Generation Z, actively adapts to the new lifestyle of the new generation of consumers, and leads young people's consumption with innovative experiences.
Positioning itself as an international high-end beer brand, it expands new drinking scenarios and actively integrates into all aspects of young consumers' lives through the experience of beer, daily life, creative scenes and other scenarios.

[Brand Interpretation]

The blue symbolizes the rising sun, which is bright and hopeful. It also echoes the word "Light" in the brand's English name, showing the youthful and energetic brand tone of Budweiser Blue. "Blue" is the brand color, combining the youthful and stylish blue with the brand tone, fully demonstrating Budweiser Blue's confident, playful and creative brand attitude.
